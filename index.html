The Classic Margarita

This cheeky cocktail is sweet and bitter with lots of character and citrus! Here is what you need:

  • Tequila
  • Lime Juice
  • Cointreau (or Triple Sec)
  • Salt (for the rim)
  • Simple Syrup

In a cocktail shaker, add 3 shots of tequila, 2 shots of lime juice and 1 shot of Cointreau. This is the 3-2-1 method and is easy to remember! Add simple syrup to taste - you might decide it doesn't need any. Cover the rim with lime juice (around 1cm) and coat with salt. Sit back and enjoy.

The Classy Cosmopolitan

Sharp and Fruity, this cocktail will give you a spring in your step

  • Vodka
  • Cranberry Juice
  • Cointreau (or Triple Sec)
  • Ice
  • Fresh Lime Juice
  • Orange Peel

Making the perfect Cosmopolitan is easy! Here is a recipie for four (or one if you are extra thirsty): Mix 8 shots of vodka (Cheapest is fine), 2 shots of Cointreau, 3 shots of Cranberry Juice (for a healthy option consider a sugar free alternative), and 2 tablespoons of lime juice in to a cocktail shaker. Add ice to the cocktail shaker and shake well. To make your drink more presentable add a twist of orange peel to garnish.

The Cousin of Aperol Spritz: Hugo

The citrusy relative of the classic summertime beverage, Aperol Spritz, please welcome the Hugo Spritz!

  • Eldeflower Liqueur
  • Sprig of Mint
  • Prosecco
  • Soda Water
  • Lemon Wheel

Add the Eldeflower Liqueur (also known as St. Germain) and the mint sprig into a wine glass and let this rest (after a good muddle!) for around 3 minutes. Then add prosecco, ice and soda water and stir evenly to combine. Add the lemon wheel for a garnish and optional add an extra mint sprig.

When life gives you lemons, make a cocktail
